Class: ItemKey

bookworm. ItemKey

The ItemKey class identifies item entity nodes in the cache. `ItemKey` subclasses `FieldKey` so that any method that accepts `FieldKey` as argument, would also accept `ItemKey`. Whatever works with fields, should also work with collection items.
